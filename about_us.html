ABOUT RAGE CAGE

Rage Cage will be located at Purok 3, Barangay Bibirao, Daet, Camarines Norte, and will offer relief, providing a safe outlet for individuals dealing with emotions like anger, stress, and frustrations. The business will cater to solo clients and groups with a maximum of 5 people per session, offering three package options: the Smash Room, the Screaming Room, and the Quiet Room. First is the smash room, where they can break the bottle and feel satisfaction and loosen their frustration every time they break it into pieces. Second is the scream room, where they can unleash all their frustration, resentment, and grudges in the soundproofed room and write and draw in the freedom wall. Third is the quiet room, where they can take a break from the noise. They can meditate, reflect, or enjoy the moment of silence.

The waiver's content will indicate that the customer acknowledges and accepts all associated risks, including medical conditions, and the release of liability waiver of claims and indemnity agreement along with the photos and video consent. Rage Cage will operate from Tuesday to Sunday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and accommodate booking online and walk-ins.
